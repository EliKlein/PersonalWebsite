Regular mode:
Match cards that have the same number and same suit color.
For example, the nine of hearts and the nine of diamonds are a match.

Complicated mode:
Match cards based on what the image in the corner shows. Cards will disappear completely, and slide upwards to fill empty spaces.


This symbol means you need to match two cards with the same number.
For example, the ace of spaces matches the ace of diamonds, the ace of clubs, or the ace of hearts.




This symbol means you need to match two cards with the same suit.
Any two cards of the same suit will do, the numbers don't need to match.
The suit colors do not matter in this mode, only their shapes.


If there are no more matches to be made, a few new cards will fall in.
